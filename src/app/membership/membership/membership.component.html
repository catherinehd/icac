Membership

ChinaICAC is an organization consists of professionals serving Chinese secondary students transitioning to higher education. Membership in ChinaICAC is now only open to school based counselors in China and college/university admission people and educators.

Types of Membership
Your member type would be… If you work for… And work as..
College/University Membership A College or University or government department Admission Officer/educator
Secondary School Membership A Secondary/High School School based Counselor
What to know before you apply:
1 All memberships are free (by Janurary of 2019) but subject to approval. Once your membership application has been approved you will be notified via email.
2 Member benefits will begin once your application is approved.
3 ChinaICAC's membership cycle is Jan. 1 through Dec. 31. If joining ChinaICAC from Jan. 1 through Sept. 30 your membership will expire Dec. 31 of the same year. Those joining after Oct. 1 membership will expire Dec. 31 of the following year.
4 Prior to membership of ChinaICAC, a secondary school counselor should work in a school with College Board CEEB code, and own membership of International ACAC.
